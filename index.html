How much does pressure washing cost? +

Most driveways run $150–$300 and full home exteriors $300–$600, depending on size and condition. We always give a free, upfront quote with no surprises.

Will pressure washing damage my home? +

Never. We use professional soft-wash techniques on delicate surfaces like roofs and siding, and adjust pressure for every material — so you get a deep clean with zero damage.

How soon can you come out? +

We offer same-week service across Miami-Dade, and often next-day. Reach out and we'll find a time that works for you.

Are you licensed and insured? +

Absolutely. Under Pressure is fully licensed and insured, so your property is 100% protected on every job.
